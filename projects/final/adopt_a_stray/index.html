Adopting a dog at ColumbiaStraySavers offers a unique opportunity to change a life, both for you and your future four-legged friend. By choosing to adopt, you're providing a loving home to a dog in need, giving them a chance for a brighter future. We are committed to the welfare of these animals and ensures they are well-cared for and ready to become part of your family. When you adopt, you not only gain a loyal and loving companion but also contribute to the broader mission of reducing pet overpopulation and supporting animal rescue efforts. Please consider welcoming a deserving dog into your and your family's life!

Adoption Process

Step 1: Fill Out the Adoption Application

After finding the right dog online, you will recieve an adoption application from us. This application often includes questions about your living situation, experience with pets, and your plans for the adopted dog.

Step 2: Approval/ Meet and Greet

If your adoption application is approved, we will work with you to schedule an opportunity to meet the dog of your choosing in a controlled environment. We do this in order to assess compatability between you and the dog

Step 3: Adoption Contract and Payment

Once all parties agree on a specific dog, we'll have you sign an adoption agreement form. This outlines the responsibilites of both parties, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other care requirements. You may also be required to pay any fees if necessary.

Step 4: Congratulations!

After all the paperwork is finished, it's time for the fun part - taking your new dog home! Ensure that you have all the supplies he or she needs; including food, bowls, leash, and a bed for your newest addition to the family.
