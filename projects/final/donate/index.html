Why Donate?

This shelter is dedicated to the well-being of every canine resident, providing them with love, care, and a second chance at a happy, healthy life. When you adopt from ColumbiaStraySavers, you're supporting their mission to rescue and rehabilitate dogs in need, while also gaining a loyal and loving companion who will bring joy, loyalty, and unwavering companionship into your home. Choosing adoption is a win-win, offering a deserving dog a forever home and opening your heart to a new, cherished member of your family.

Ways to Donate:

  • Mail a Check
  • Make checks payable to "Columbia Stray Savers" and mail them to 156 Any Street, Washington, DC, 20500
  • Supply Donations
  • CAN BE DROPPED OFF AT OUR FACILITY

Recurring Donation

Setting up a recurring monthly donation to support the shelter is a meaningful way to ensure a steady source of income for our vital work. By committing to a monthly contribution, you can help us continue our mission of rescuing and caring for dogs, making a lasting and positive impact on the lives of countless animals in need.
