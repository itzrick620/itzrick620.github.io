Our Mission

At ColumbiaStraySavers, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray and abandoned animals, with a special focus on dogs and cats. We are committed to providing a safe haven, compassionate care, and unwavering support to these animals, working tirelessly to give them a chance for a brighter future. Through our dedicated efforts and community engagement, we aim to reduce pet overpopulation, promote responsible pet ownership, and create a world where every animal is cherished and protected. Our ultimate goal is to save and improve the lives of these innocent creatures, one rescue at a time, as we strive for a compassionate and more humane world for all.
